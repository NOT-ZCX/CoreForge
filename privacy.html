CoreFoge Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CoreForge collects, uses, and stores data when you interact with the CoreForge Discord bot and related services.

1. Information We Collect

CoreForge may collect and store the following types of information as part of normal operation:

  • Discord server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and user IDs.
  • Server configuration and settings for CoreForge features.
  • Command usage data (such as which commands are used and when).
  • Log data related to errors, performance, and system health.

CoreForge does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information beyond what is provided by Discord or required for functionality.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Provide CoreForge’s features and services within your server.
  • Maintain configuration persistence between restarts.
  • Monitor performance, detect issues, and improve stability.
  • Analyze feature usage to improve and refine CoreForge.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored securely using reasonable technical measures. Configuration and usage data may be retained for as long as CoreForge is active in a server and for a reasonable period afterward for maintenance and analytics, unless otherwise requested or removed.

4. Log Data

CoreForge may generate internal logs containing technical details such as timestamps, event types, and error messages. These logs are used strictly for debugging, security, and performance monitoring.

5. Third-Party Services

CoreForge operates primarily via Discord’s platform and may rely on third-party hosting, databases, or monitoring tools. These services may process data on our behalf but are required to follow reasonable security practices.

6. Cookies and Website Tracking

If CoreForge is accompanied by a website or dashboard, standard web analytics may be used (such as IP address, browser type, or page views). Any such tracking is used to understand usage trends and improve the service.

7. Data Sharing

CoreForge does not sell user data. Data may be shared only in limited cases such as:

  • Compliance with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.
  • Protecting the security, integrity, or rights of CoreForge, its users, or the public.
  • Service providers who assist with hosting, storage, or analytics under appropriate safeguards.

8. Your Choices and Data Removal

You can remove CoreForge from your Discord server at any time. This may result in eventual deletion of server-specific configuration data. If you require specific data removal or have privacy concerns, you can contact the CoreForge team through official support channels.

9. Children’s Privacy

CoreForge is intended for use within Discord, which has its own age requirements. CoreForge does not knowingly target or collect information from individuals under the minimum age required by Discord’s Terms of Service.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of CoreForge after changes are published indicates your acceptance of the updated policy. The “Last Updated” date reflects the latest version.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or data handling practices, contact the CoreForge team through the official support server or listed communication channels.
